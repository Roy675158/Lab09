Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Orange Juice
  • 2 Cups of Tequila
  • 1 Cup Cranberry Juice
  • 1 Cup Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Cup Ginger Ale
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Pour 2 cuprs of orange juice into a large pitcher
  2. Pour 2 cups of your favorite Tequila
  3. Add 1 cup of Cranberry Juice
  4. Add 1 cup of Ginger ale soda
  5. Mix ingredients well then taste
  6. Add whipped cream on top
